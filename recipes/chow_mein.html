Chow Mein

Description

Chow Mein are Chinese stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu.This dish is popular throughout the Chinese diaspora and appears on the menus of mos Chinese restaurants abroad.

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast
  • 4 cups green cabbage
  • 1.5 tbsp peanut oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 200g chow mein noodles
  • 1 carrot
  • 1.5 cups bean sprouts
  • 3 green onions
  • 1/4 cup water

CHOW MEIN SAUCE

  • 2 tsp cornflower
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp chinese cooking wine
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • white pepper

Preperation

  1. Marinate Chicken
  2. Prepare the noodles according to the packet instructions and then drain
  3. Mix the cornflour and soy sauce together, then mix the remaining ingredients
  4. Heat oil in wok or large fry pan over high heat
  5. Add garlic and stir fry for 10 seconcds or until it starts to turn golden
  6. Add chicken and stir fry until the surface gets a tinge of browning but inside is still raw (about 1 minuute)
  7. Add the cabbage, carrot, and the white pices of shallots. Stir
  8. Add the noodles, sauce and water. Stir fry for 1 minute, tossing constantly.
  9. Add bean sprouts and remaining shallots/scallions. Toss through for 30 seconds or until the bean sprouts just start to wilt.
  10. Remove from heat and serve immediatley. Enjoy!

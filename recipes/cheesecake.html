Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers. The main, and thickest, layer sonsists of a mixture of a soft, fresh cheese, eggs, and sugar. If there is a bottom layer, it most often consists of a crust or base made from crushed cookies, graham crackers, pastry, or sometimes sponge cake. Cheesecake may be baked or unbaked.

Ingredients

FOR THE CRUST
  • graham crackers
  • butter
  • sugar
  • salt
FOR THE CHEESECAKE FILLING
  • cream cheese
  • sugar
  • sour cream
  • vanilla extract
  • eggs

Preperation

  1. crush the graham crackers until they turn to crumbs
  2. mix the crumbs with melted butter, granulated sugar, and a little kosher salt.
  3. press the mix into the bottom and halfay up the sides of a 9-inch springform pan.
  4. pre-bake the crust for 8-10 minutes
  5. mix all the cheesecake filling ingredients
  6. pour the cheesecake filling on top of the crust and spread evenly
  7. bake the cheesecake for about 60-70 minutes or until the center is almost set
  8. allow the cheesecake to cool at room temperature
  9. add red fruits and whipcream if you want, enjoy!

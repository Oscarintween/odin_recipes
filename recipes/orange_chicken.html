Orange Chicken

Description

Orange Chicken is a Chinese-American dish that blends sweet, sour, and citrous flavors to create a one of a kind flavour.

The main ingredient of course is Chicken. It is first covered in batter and then fried.

The orange sauce is a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, orange zest and crushed crushed red peppers that thicken in a pan with deep fried battered chicken with will help thicken the sauce, It is super easy to make.

Ingredients

CHICKEN
  • 2 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 egg
  • salt
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoons oil
ORANGE SAUCE
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili flake
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons corn starch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Preperation

  1. Cut chicken into 1x1-inch cubes
  2. combine salt, white pepper, cornstarch, and flour in a bowl
  3. Add the egg, 1.5 cups of water, and oil until it is all mixed well
  4. Add the chicken to the batter and refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a wok or heacy bottom pan to 350ËšF
  6. Gently add theh chicken and cook for 5-6 minutes until golden brown
  7. Remove the chicken onced fried and set aside
  8. Set pot over medium-high heat and add the oil
  9. Once oil begins to shimmer, add the red pepper flakes, ginger, and garlic, and cook for 30 seconds, stirring constantly
  10. Add the sugar and brown sugar, and stir
  11. Add the orange juice and allow the sugars to begin to dissolbe in the liquied
  12. Add the vinegar and soy sauce
  13. Mix the cornstarch with water and then Add to the pot and contiue to stir
  14. Once mix has consistency of syrup add the chicken and mix
